# Privacy Policy

This service processes user-provided project content to draft, approve, and create Jira tickets.

## Data We Process
- Module descriptions, ticket drafts, and approval messages.
- Technical metadata required to operate the API.

## How Data Is Used
- To generate draft tickets.
- To record approvals.
- To create issues in Atlassian Jira when explicitly approved.

## Data Sharing
- Data may be sent to Atlassian Jira for ticket creation.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Retention
- Minimal operational logs and local state may be retained for workflow reliability.
